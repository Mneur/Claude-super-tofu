PAIN POINTS & SOLUTIONS
1
Wireless keyboards die mid-tournament — and the charging cable is always somewhere else.
Solution: 5000 mAh cell + auto-fallback. 2.4 G drops, it switches to BT in 200 ms. Battery low, plug C-to-A and keep playing wired — zero downtime.
2
Cheap ABS keycaps shine into mirrors after a month of heavy use.
Solution: Genuine PBT double-shot. Sand-blasted matte texture rated for 3 M+ keystrokes; legends are part of the plastic, can't wear off.
3
One dead switch and the whole board becomes e-waste — no repair option for the buyer.
Solution: Tool-free hot-swap sockets. Lift cap, pull switch, push new one in — 10 seconds per key. 4 spare switches in the box.
4
RGB looks cheap because north-facing LEDs leak around the keycap instead of through the legend.
Solution: South-facing per-key SMD LEDs. Reads cleanly through Cherry & OEM keycap legends. No more "halo" leakage — premium feel at ¥199.
5
2.4 G dongles always feel a beat behind wired — gamers refuse to commit.
Solution: 1000 Hz polling RF + low-latency chip. Click-to-photon under 4 ms — indistinguishable from wired in blind A/B tests.
6
Switching one keyboard between laptop, phone, and tablet means re-pairing every single time.
Solution: Tri-mode + 3 paired BT slots. Fn+1/2/3 snaps between three devices instantly. One board, the whole desk.
