## 😖 Pain Points & Solutions (6 items, left column of lower section)

```
1. Problem: "Premium" keyboards still ship with cheap plastic tops — they ruin a thousand-dollar desk setup.
   Solution: Acoustic knit fabric top — turns the most visible surface into a designed object that fits a premium desk.

2. Problem: Switching between MacBook, iPad, and phone wastes minutes on Bluetooth re-pairing every time.
   Solution: Side mode-knob + BT 5.3 with 3-device memory — twist once, one-second context switching, no Fn combos.

3. Problem: Keycap legends fade after a year — your "premium" board ends up looking three years old.
   Solution: PBT double-shot keycaps — legends molded through the cap, physically cannot fade. Arrow keys dye-sublimated in color.

4. Problem: Mid-meeting volume, mute, and time-check all require Fn combos or removing hands from keys.
   Solution: Dual knobs + LED matrix clock — three high-frequency desktop actions become physical and visual.

5. Problem: Most wireless keyboards die in a day with RGB on — at that point, why bother going wireless?
   Solution: 8000 mAh — largest battery in its class. 40 hrs backlit, 200 hrs lights-off. A spec number that sells.

6. Problem: Mechanical keyboards sound thin and hollow — cheap acoustics kill any premium feel.
   Solution: Gasket mount + 4-layer dampening with KTT Misty Blue linear switches. Deep "thock" — quality you can hear.
```
