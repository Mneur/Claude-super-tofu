## ⭐ 6 Unique Features

```
1. RED · Acoustic Knit Top — A Keyboard That Belongs on a Premium Desk
   Fabric-finished top panel with embedded LED matrix display. The signature visual language of SUPER MAXFIT — premium texture where every other keyboard shows plastic.

2. LED Matrix Display · 7×33 Pixels with Live Clock
   Built-in pixel display shows time by default, fully customizable via web driver — your desk gains a private information screen.

3. Dual Smart Knobs · Volume + Mode
   Large knob for volume, small knob for device/mode switching. High-frequency actions become physical and instant.

4. KTT Misty Blue Linear · 43gf · 50M Lifespan
   Light linear feel tuned for all-day typing and gaming. Misty blue / purple POM stems — even the switches look premium.

5. 8000 mAh · Up to 200-Hour Battery
   Largest battery in its class. Backlit gaming still lasts a full work week (40 hours); lights-off mode hits 200 hours.

6. Gasket Mount + 4-Layer Sound Dampening
   Plate foam + IXPE + TPR + bottom foam, paired with gasket-floating mount for that deep "thock" — quality you can hear.
```

## 😖 Pain Points & Solutions

```
1. "Premium" keyboards still ship with cheap plastic tops — they ruin a thousand-dollar desk setup.
→ Acoustic knit fabric top — turns the most visible surface into a designed object that fits a premium desk.

2. Switching between MacBook, iPad, and phone wastes minutes on Bluetooth re-pairing every time.
→ Side mode-knob + BT 5.3 with 3-device memory — twist once, one-second context switching, no Fn combos.

3. Keycap legends fade after a year — your "premium" board ends up looking three years old.
→ PBT double-shot keycaps — legends molded through the cap, physically cannot fade. Arrow keys dye-sublimated in color.

4. Mid-meeting volume, mute, and time-check all require Fn combos or removing hands from keys.
→ Dual knobs + LED matrix clock — three high-frequency desktop actions become physical and visual.

5. Most wireless keyboards die in a day with RGB on — at that point, why bother going wireless?
→ 8000 mAh — largest battery in its class. 40 hrs backlit, 200 hrs lights-off. A spec number that sells.

6. Mechanical keyboards sound thin and hollow — cheap acoustics kill any premium feel.
→ Gasket mount + 4-layer dampening with KTT Misty Blue linear switches. Deep "thock" — quality you can hear.
```
